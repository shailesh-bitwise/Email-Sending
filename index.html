Important Information

To get your app password:

  • For Gmail:
  • 1. Enable 2-Step Verification:
  • 2. Go to your Google Account.
  • 3. Select "Security" in the navigation panel.
  • 4. Under "Signing in to Google," select "2-Step Verification" and set it up.
  • Generate an App Password:
  • 1. After enabling 2-Step Verification, go back to the "Security" section of your Google Account.
  • 2. Under "Signing in to Google," select "App passwords."
  • 3. You might need to sign in again.
  • 4. At the bottom, choose "Select app" and choose the app you’re using (or select "Other (Custom name)" and enter your custom app name).
  • 5. Select "Generate."
  • 6. You’ll see a 16-character app password. Copy this password.
  • 7. Paste it in the App password text box.
